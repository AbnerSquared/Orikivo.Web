What does Arcadia do?
A wide assortment of things. Look below to get a better insight on each core mechanic.
Multiplayer
As one of the primary focal points for this bot, Arcadia is equipped to support game servers with multiple channel connections!
Chess
A classic strategy game that challenges even the wisest. Tough it out with a worthy opponent.
Trivia
A casual game where a small group of people can all answer questions about random things.
Werewolf (Beta)
A social manipulation game where the villagers have to eliminate the werewolves before they all become a feast.
Casino
A place commonly used to attract the desperate in need of Orite. While some games offer rewards never seen by a living soul, one must keep in mind the risks that may follow. Falling into debt is no laughing matter. On the other hand, we have a growing assortment of modes!
Blackjack
A game mode that provides a mix of strategy alongside luck. Pave the way to profitable success!
Doubler
An interesting mode that allows you to guess where the tick is going to stop at. For each successful tick, your bet is doubled!
Gimi
A common 'give or take' approach that uses Orite instead. You have a random chance to win or lose a lot.
Roulette
A mode that allows you to bet in a wide variety of styles. A random slot is selected, and based on your style, you can earn plenty of chips!
